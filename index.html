Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Information We Collect

ShopSmarter collects your email address and password when you create an account. We also store your saved items, favorite stores, and coverage preferences to sync across your devices.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provide the ShopSmarter service — syncing your preferences and saved items, personalizing your feed, and sending you relevant updates if you opt in. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Third-Party Services

ShopSmarter uses Firebase for authentication and data storage, and OpenAI's API to analyze product images. We use affiliate links from partner networks — when you purchase through our app, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you.
